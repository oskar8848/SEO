Conclusion

Implementing SEOF across 50% of Seneca's campuses boosts energy efficiency, supports sustainability goals, and significantly reduces emissions

Implementing SEOF across 50% of Seneca’s campuses addresses critical energy inefficiencies while aligning with institutional sustainability goals. IoT technologies enable proactive energy management (Pérez-Lombard et al., 2021), while phased retrofitting offers a proven return on investment (Ürge-Vorsatz et al., 2020). By integrating SEOF with renewable energy systems, Seneca can reduce emissions by 50% (Alanne & Saari, 2022), reinforcing its leadership in sustainability.
